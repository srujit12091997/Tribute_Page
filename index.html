Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug, American agronomist, humanitarian, and Nobel laureate.

Dr. Norman Ernest Borlaug was an American agronomist who led initiatives worldwide that contributed to the extensive increases in agricultural production termed the Green Revolution. He was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize for his lifetime of work to feed a hungry world.

Borlaug's efforts are credited with saving over a billion people from starvation. His methods continue to impact food production and agricultural policy today.
